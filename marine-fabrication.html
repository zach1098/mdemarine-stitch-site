What to collect

Before someone asks for fabrication help

  • Photos from multiple angles, especially if the fabricated piece needs to match an existing install
  • Dimensions, measurements, or even rough hand sketches if no drawing exists
  • Material preference if known, otherwise describe where the part lives and what it supports
  • Whether the part is cosmetic, structural, or tied to an engine installation
  • Whether the job is standalone or part of a larger engine or yard project
