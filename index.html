What to include in a service request

Make it easy to scope the job. The more specific the request, the faster someone can tell whether it is a dockside call, a yard project, or a full repower conversation.

  • Vessel details: make, model, length, and current location
  • Engine details: brand, model, horsepower, and hours if known
  • Problem summary: no-start, overheating, smoke, vibration, leak, or planned replacement
  • Timing: whether the boat is down now or the work is being scheduled ahead
  • Photos: engine room, damaged parts, gauges, alarms, and access points
